Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats does ExamBlox support?

We support PDF, DOCX, TXT, JPG, and PNG files. Our AI can extract text from these formats to generate relevant questions.

How accurate are the generated questions?

Our AI has been trained on educational materials to generate highly relevant and accurate questions. The accuracy depends on the quality of the source material, but users consistently report 90%+ relevance.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and never store your files after processing. Your materials are completely private and secure.

Can I use ExamBlox for commercial purposes?

Our free plan is for personal use only. For commercial use, please subscribe to our Premium plan or contact us for institutional licensing.
